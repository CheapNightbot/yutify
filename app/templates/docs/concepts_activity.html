The metadata / information about a user's currently listening music on a linked service(s). This guide explains how to link a service to your account and access your activity data.

To link a service to your account, first Login or Sign Up. Then, go to your {% if current_user.is_authenticated %} Settings{% else %} "Settings" {% endif %} page and scroll to the "Services" section. Now click on the "Link" button next to the service you want to link and follow the instructions.

After successfully linking a service, you will be redirected back to the settings page where you can see the linked service. And for this example, I linked my Spotify account:

Now that you have linked a service, listen to some music on that service and then go to your {% if current_user.is_authenticated %}Profile{% else %}"Profile"{% endif %} page, you should see the metadata about your currently listening music:

You can also access your activity data by making a request to the /api/me endpoint. This endpoint will return the metadata of your currently listening music on the linked service(s).
